Avatar: The Way of Water

A 2022 sci-fi film directed by James Cameron. It follows Jake Sully and his family as they flee their home and explore the oceans of Pandora.

Images

Top 5 Key Events in the Movie

  1. Jake and Neytiri raise their children on Pandora.
  2. The RDA returns with a new Na'vi version of Colonel Quaritch.
  3. The Sully family flees and joins the Metkayina reef clan.
  4. The RDA hunts Tulkuns for a life-extending serum.
  5. A big battle happens on the RDA ship — and the ocean becomes home.

Main Characters

  • Jake Sully – leader of the Omatikaya
  • Neytiri – Na'vi warrior
  • Lo'ak – Jake's son, loves the ocean
  • Kiri – adopted daughter, connected to Eywa
  • Spider – human boy raised among Na'vi
  • Payakan – outcast Tulkun, Lo'ak's friend

Cool Facts

  • Released on December 16, 2022
  • Earned over $2.3 billion worldwide
  • Won the Academy Award for Best Visual Effects
  • The cast learned to freedive for underwater scenes
  • The movie is 3 hours and 12 minutes long
